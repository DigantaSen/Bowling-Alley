Bowling Rules (SOOK)

Basic Rules

  • Goal: Knock down all ten pins
  • Each frame: Two balls to knock down all pins
  • Strike: All pins knocked down with first ball (marked as X)
  • Spare: All pins knocked down with second ball (marked as /)
  • Game consists of 10 frames
  • 10th frame special: Strike = 2 bonus balls, Spare = 1 bonus ball

Scoring

  • Score = Total pins knocked down
  • Strike: 10 + next 2 balls
  • Spare: 10 + next 1 ball
  • Open Frame: Just the pins knocked down
  • Maximum score per game: 300 (12 strikes)

Game Modes

  • Singles: 1 athlete, 3-game average (max 300)
  • Unified Doubles: 2 players (athlete + partner), combined 3-game average (max 600)
  • Unified Team: 4 players (2 athletes + 2 partners), combined 3-game average (max 1200)

Lane Etiquette

  • Bowl on your own lane
  • Don't bowl when someone next to you is bowling
  • Wear appropriate bowling shoes
